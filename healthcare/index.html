Division Preview

What the Healthcare Division Will Offer

The same compliance discipline and veteran-owned accountability that defines PSG’s IT division, applied to federal healthcare staffing for VA medical facilities nationwide.

Labor Categories

Clinical & Allied Health

Registered Nurses, Licensed Practical Nurses, Medical Support Assistants, Certified Nursing Assistants, Physical Therapists, Occupational Therapists, and Allied Health professionals for VA medical centers and clinics.

Compliance

SCA & Healthcare-Specific Standards

Every healthcare placement will carry the same SCA Wage Determination compliance rigor as PSG’s IT division. Healthcare-specific credentialing, licensure verification, and VA facility requirements will be built into the placement process.

Employment Model

W-2, Full Benefits

All healthcare workers will be W-2 employees of PSG with full SCA Health & Welfare fringe, federal holidays, paid vacation, and sick leave. The same model that protects IT workers will protect healthcare workers.

Coverage

All 18 VA VISNs Nationwide

National recruiting and placement across all 18 VA Veterans Integrated Service Networks. Remote telehealth roles, hybrid positions, and on-site VA medical center placements all supported under the same PSG process.
