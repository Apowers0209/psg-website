How It Works

From Position Request to Cleared Worker

PSG handles every step from receiving a position requirement to placing a cleared, SCA-compliant worker on your contract.

01

Position Review

PSG receives position requirements, pulls the applicable Wage Determination from SAM.gov, and prices the position correctly before accepting it. No stale WDs.

02

Technical Screening

The owner personally screens every candidate using role-specific technical criteria. One qualified candidate is presented - not a stack of résumés to sort through.

03

Clearance Processing

PSG sponsors the Public Trust application (SF-85P or SF-86) at no cost to the candidate. Priority given to candidates with existing clearances to reduce lead times.

04

W-2 Placement

Worker is employed on PSG W-2 payroll with full SCA benefits. PSG handles all payroll, fringe, compliance, and ongoing worker management throughout the contract.

Common Questions

Federal IT Staffing FAQ

What IT labor categories does PSG staff for VA contracts?

PSG staffs federal IT workers across five service lines: End-User Computing (Help Desk Tier 1/2/3, Desktop, VTC, Field Tech), Infrastructure Operations (SysAdmin, Windows/Linux, endpoint, IAM, storage), Network Operations and Engineering (NetAdmin, NE1, NE2, NOC, firewall/VPN), Cybersecurity Operations Support (CSSP/DoD 8570/8140 — SOC Analysts, CSSP Analysts, CSSP Infrastructure Support, CSSP Incident Responders, IAT Level I/II/III), and IT Service Management and Program Support (ServiceNow/ITSM Administrators, IT Project Coordinators, configuration management, O&M). All placements under NAICS 541512, 541519, and 561320.

Does PSG cover all 18 VA VISNs?

Yes. PSG recruits nationally and supports remote, hybrid, and on-site IT placements across all 18 VA Veterans Integrated Service Networks. Any state, any VISN, same process.

How does PSG handle Wage Determination compliance?

PSG pulls the applicable SCA Wage Determination from SAM.gov for each work location before accepting any position. WDs change by locality - PSG never uses a stale rate. All IT workers are paid at or above the applicable prevailing wage.

Does PSG sponsor Public Trust clearances?

Yes. PSG sponsors SF-85P and SF-86 applications at no cost to the candidate. We prioritize candidates with existing Public Trust or Secret clearances to reduce onboarding lead times for prime contractors.

What benefits do PSG IT workers receive?

All PSG IT workers receive W-2 employment, SCA H&W fringe at $5.55/hr, 11 paid federal holidays, 80 hours paid vacation annually, EO 13706 paid sick leave, and Public Trust clearance sponsorship.

How quickly does PSG respond to new position requests?

PSG is owner-operated with no layers. Position requests are acknowledged within hours, not days. A pre-screened candidate can typically be presented within 5 to 10 business days depending on clearance requirements and labor category.
